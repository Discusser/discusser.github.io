Warframe


Method 1:

The easiest way to farm affinity as a beginner is by doing Sanctuary Onslaught. This game mode can be found in the Syndicate section of the star chart. This game mode is very good for new players because you can do it at any warframe level and the enemies are at a relatively low level.


Alternatively, you could try doing Elite Sanctuary Onslaught (right under Sanctuary Onslaught) which is a higher level version of Sanctuary Onslaught. The enemies have a much higher level, but you get a lot more affinity. Unfortunately you cannot do it without a level 30 warframe unless your mastery rank is 30.
